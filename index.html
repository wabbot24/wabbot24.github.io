About Me

This is my bio. It is an amazing bio. All who read my bio weep tears of joy and throw themselves upon the ground in anguished ecstacy. Perhaps you too are already incapacitated by the beauty of these words - fear not! It is only upon first exposure that these reactions linger. All you must do to attain sustainable bliss is hire me to a job offering a lucrative salary with benefits. Please give me health insurance. If I'm not healthy, I cannot continue to bring such joy to the world.

It's impossible to understate how important it is to have good words in here. This is how people will create a first impression of me and my work. For now though, I'm putting in a string of whatever nonsense comes into my head, including this meta-nonsense about the nonsense held within this paragraph. Hopefully one day I'll have good words - the best words - to populate this section, but for now they remain the thoughtless drivel of a thoughtless driveler.

I do hope to be forgiven for this nonsense, but I understand if you find it tedious and decline to offer me a lucrative salary and benefits. But mark my words - though now they may be lacking, one day I'll have better ones.
